About us

Meet the Family

We're a tight knit family passionate about bringing people together through delicious food and heartfelt service. With each dish lovingly prepared and every event organised, we hope all our customers feel the authenticity that comes with being a part of our family. We are from the Philippines, specifically Negros Oriental in the Visayan Islands and want to spread the spirit of Filipino food to our friends in Sydney. Our dishes are more than just meals, they honour traditions and celebrate culture. The moment you taste our cooking, you are savouring our secret recipes, the beauty of our heritage and the comfort of our home.
